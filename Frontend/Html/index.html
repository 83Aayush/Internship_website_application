Our Story

Rooted in Tradition,
Refined by Craft

Artius Interior Private Limited was founded with a singular belief — that the spaces we live in should be as carefully crafted as the lives we live within them. From our workshop, we transform premium hardwoods into bespoke doors, windows, panelling, and furniture.

Every piece bears the mark of a skilled artisan. We combine time-honoured joinery with modern precision, delivering woodwork that is both enduring and deeply beautiful.
